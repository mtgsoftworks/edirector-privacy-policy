Privacy Policy

eDirector - Football Manager Game

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 26, 2025

Quick Summary: eDirector is a football manager simulation game developed by MTG Softworks. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Introduction

Welcome to eDirector! MTG Softworks ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the eDirector mobile application (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use eDirector, you may provide the following information:

  • Coach Name: A name you choose for your in-game coach profile
  • Game Progress: Your team selections, game saves, match results, and other gameplay data
  • Game Settings: Language preferences and other app settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do NOT collect any analytics, tracking data, or device information. eDirector is designed to work completely offline without any external data transmission.

3. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is stored locally on your device and used solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the game functionality
  • Saving your game progress and preferences
  • Remembering your settings and configurations
  • Enabling game features such as multiple save slots

Important: We do not transmit, share, or upload any of your data to external servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your game data is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage mechanisms (localStorage). This includes:

  • Game saves (up to 10 save slots)
  • Coach profiles and team selections
  • Game progress and statistics
  • App settings and preferences

4.2 Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's security features. We recommend:

  • Using device screen lock and security features
  • Regularly backing up your device
  • Being cautious when sharing your device with others

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for any purpose. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to your information.

6. Third-Party Services

eDirector does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently without requiring internet connectivity.

7. Children's Privacy

eDirector is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and no information is transmitted to us, we have no access to any user data, including children's data.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Data: You can delete your game saves at any time through the app's interface
  • Clear All Data: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • Control Settings: You can change language and other preferences at any time
  • Export Data: While the app doesn't have a built-in export feature, your save files are stored locally on your device

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete specific save files through the app
  • Clear the app's data through device settings
  • Uninstall the application

We do not have any data retention policies as we do not store any data on our servers.

10. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers involved in using eDirector.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notifying you through the app (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

12. Permissions

eDirector requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save game progress and settings locally on your device
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline
  • No Camera/Microphone: We do not access these features
  • No Location: We do not track your location
